Game Summary

Pokemon GO is a mobile game requiring the player to leave their couch/bedroom and go outside in order to play.
Due to this need to go outside, players began to roam their streets and parks looking for Pokemon, and as more people joined in the game continued to grow.

The game revolves around the player utilizing the "augmented reality" feature of pointing their camera around in order to find and catch a Pokemon, once the player has caught a Pokemon they may use them in order to battle and capture gyms.

Elemental Aspects

Pokemon GO is indeed based around the Pokemon universe, which is widely known for its long and indepth list of Elements.
Elements are handled in a way that requires the player to strategize over which Pokemon of which Element to use due to the game's focus on the "strength and weaknesses" system of tackling elements.
This means that a Fire Pokemon will typically get beaten by a Water Pokemon, or a Grass Pokemon will get beat by a Fire Pokemon.
