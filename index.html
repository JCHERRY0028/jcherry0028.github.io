What is this website's focus?

Elementally is a website focused on finding and detailing video games that have content and gameplay aspects revolved around or made up of the popular romanticization of elements; Ice, Water, Earth, Electricity, and Fire.
Although these were listed, they are not the only included ones.
Games that include other elements will likely have them included here if it is a major aspect of the game.

What types of Games?

Every type of game should find its way on to the website as long as it fits the requirement of containing some element focus.
These games will then be categorized based on what platform it is on. For example, Skyrim would be placed under "PC" and "Console" due
to being on both platforms.
For all mobile platforms (Nintendo 3DS/DS, PS vita, etc.), they will be under the "Mobile" category.
