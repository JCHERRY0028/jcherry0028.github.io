Game Summary

Skyrim is based on a lone adventurer, the player, who may complete quests and fight against a horde of resurrected dragons as well as their leader, Alduin, in order to restore order to the world.

Game Play

Elemental Aspects

Skyrim is full of magic, weaponry, armory, and many other miscellaneous components.
Due to this, the game has included many elemental aspects to impress and entertain the player.
Unfortunately, while there are forms of other elements seen in the game the only ones that seemed to have gotten a large amount of focus were; Ice, Electricity/Lightning, and Fire.
This was likely done in order to simplify the amount of magic spells, in this case specifically the "destruction" kind, and to not have an overwhelming number of them.

While Console and Mobile versions (Nintento Switch) cannot utilize game modifications, unless a console player has purchased Skyrim: Special Edition, whereas PC players with any version of the game may download modifications freely or through transactions.
With these modifications, players may add in content of their choosing, allowing them to shape the game as they see fit and include whatever elements that didn't make the cut.
