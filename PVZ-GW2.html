Game Summary

Plants vs. Zombies: Garden Warfare 2 is based around the Plants vs. Zombies universe where zombies have taken over the world and plants have grown and adapted to fight the zombies off while protecting their owners. In this game, however, you play as either the plants or the zombies in a third person shooter player-versus-player battleground.

Game Play

Elemental Aspects

Plants vs. Zombies: Garden Warfare 2 is full of character with differing playstyles, from close-range melee attackers to long-range snipers, the game has a character to fit each role. Among these characters are variants which are differences to the same character.
For example, the cactus has its normal form, as well as an Ice form, Electric form, Fire form, etc.

The game handles Elemental aspects very well, with Ice having attacks slow and eventually freezing enemies, Electric attacks that arc between enemies, Fire attacks causing lasting burn effects, and Toxic attacks that cause lasting damaging to enemies, but unlike Fire is weaker and longer-lasting.

For those wanting "strengths and weaknesses" between each Element, this is not the game for you.
In order to ensure fairness and the keep combat from being overly-complicated, Elements will cause their intended effect no matter what Element the target is.
